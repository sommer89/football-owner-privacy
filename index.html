Football Owner — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 23 June 2026

Short version: Football Owner does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. The game runs entirely on your device, works fully offline, contains no ads, no analytics, no account system, and no third-party tracking.

This Privacy Policy explains how the Football Owner mobile application (the “App”), developed by Sebastian Akselsen (“we”, “us”), handles information. By using the App you agree to this policy.

Information we collect

None. The App does not ask for, collect, or process any personal information. There is no sign-up or login, and we do not gather usage analytics, advertising identifiers, location, contacts, or any other data about you or your device.

How your game data is stored

Your game progress (saved careers) is stored only on your device, in the App’s private storage area. We have no access to it. If you use Apple’s device or iCloud backup, your saves may be included in that backup under your own Apple account, governed by Apple’s Privacy Policy — not by us. You can remove all game data at any time by deleting the App.

Network access

The App does not connect to the internet and sends no information off your device. It embeds no third-party software development kits (SDKs), advertising networks, or analytics services.

Tracking

We do not track you. The App does not monitor your activity across other apps or websites and does not use the App Tracking Transparency framework because there is nothing to track.

Children’s privacy

The App is suitable for general audiences and collects no data from anyone, including children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children of any age.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new “Last updated” date.
